FreeGram - Free Programming Lessons

Learn programming for free, at your own pace. No paywalls. No pressure. Just pure learning and fun projects!

Welcome to the landing page for FreeGram! I've been interested in learning programming for a while, and have given a bunch of different websites a fair shot. There are absolutely a lot of solid ressources online, but knowing where to start and how to progress isn't always easy. The most frustrating experience I've had multiple times is finding a website that seems great, only to get hit with a paywall after just a short hour of learning.

Of course, there are fantastic resources like freeCodeCamp and MDN to mention just a few, but people learn in different ways, and I like learning by doing, so just reading or watching videos isn't really what I'm interested in.

This website will be a personal learning project. A way for me to learn more HTML, CSS, JS, Python, SQL and C++, and more. It will take a long time, but I hope to at some point be able to provide a course or three, starting with basic Python. So, stay tuned! You can follow the progress on GitHub, and feel free to submitt issues, though I'm currently not looking for contributions.
